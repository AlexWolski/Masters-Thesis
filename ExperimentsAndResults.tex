\section{Partial~Reconstruction~Refinement}
\label{sec:partial_recon_experiment}

Take a mesh, occlude part of the geometry, and convert it to an SDF input sample. Perhaps 10 samples from a few categories. Determine the cascade to use by generating the first refinement on all cascade models and choosing the highest. Run refinement for all remaining trained cascade models.
